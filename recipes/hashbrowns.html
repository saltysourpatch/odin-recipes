Hashbrowns

Hashbrowns.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Tabsco (optional but highly recommended)

LETS COOK

  1. Find some nice Yukon Potatoes
  2. Grate the potatoes up (the more fine the better)
  3. Squeeze out the moisture and make those potatoes dry
  4. Cook the hasbrowns at a medium-high heat with the butter already melted on the pan before adding the potatoes
  5. Flip and complete until they are a golden BROWN
