Grilled Cheese

Grilled cheese

Ingredients

  • Cheddar or Pepper Jack Cheese
  • Sourdough bread
  • Butter

Cooking Time

  1. Warm the pan to medium-high heat along with the butter
  2. Put the first piece of bread with the cheese on the pan
  3. Allow the bread to get golden brown and flip to complete the other side
  4. Make sure not to share and eat all by yourself
